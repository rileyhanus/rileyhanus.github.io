Cold Turkey and the Fruitcakes

When I was living in Chicago, my friends and we formed a folk-rock mash-up coverband named Cold Turkey and the Fruitcakes. We recorded two albums in our basement studio, yes one is a Christmas album. We were really going for that "gem on the back shelf at Goodwill" vibe.

Alex Lupo (Vocals, Guitar) Riley Hanus (Vocals, Mandolin, Guitar) Matthias Agne (Bass, Baritone) Robert Free (Drums, Percussion) Blake Rason (Violin, Viola)
