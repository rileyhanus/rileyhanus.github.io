Hello! My name is

Riley Hanus

I'm a multi-instrumentalist, singer, song-writer, and music director in the Raleigh-Durham area. I have 20 years of experience playing live music, running sound, directing bands, and recording.
In Raleigh I manage the group Riley_from_Iowa. We are a versitile group of musicians that can scale from a 1 person to a 6 peice party band. We strive to match the energy of the venue and the crowd, and can tailor our setlist from party rock, to acoustic folk/country, to piano bar. We have even gone as specific as performing a Jimmy Buffett tribute set, and 2 hours of exclusively Halloween-themed songs. So don't be bashful with your live music requests! Below, you can see as sampling of our ever-growing setlist, example live videos, and booking information.

Example Live Videos

These are example raw videos, so you can know what to expect. We can tailor our setlist to the venue and crowd, so just let us know what you're looking for!

Cold Turkey and the Fruitcakes

When I was living in Chicago, my friends and we formed a folk-rock mash-up coverband named Cold Turkey and the Fruitcakes. We recorded two albums in our basement studio, yes one is a Christmas album. We were really going for that "back shelf at Goodwill" vibe.

Alex Lupo (Vocals, Guitar) Riley Hanus (Vocals, Mandolin, Guitar) Matthias Agne (Bass, Baritone) Robert Free (Drums, Percussion) Blake Rason (Violin, Viola)

The Real Quaid

The Real Quaid started as an Ames Iowa college band. We played frequently in Ames, Des Moines, Cedar Rapids, and the Quad Cities. The glory days. We still play sometimes. When we can.

Riley Hanus (Vocals, Guitar, Piano) Matt Knoll (Vocals, Guitar) Vince Hartogh (Drums, Vocals) Colin Grace (Bass)
