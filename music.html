I play, write, and produce music. My solo act is Riley_from_Iowa. I also currently sing in the Atlanta-based coverband Almost Famous.

Cold Turkey and the Fruitcakes

When I was living in Chicago, my friends and I formed a folk-rock mash-up coverband named Cold Turkey and the Fruitcakes. We recorded two albums in our basement studio, yes one is a Christmas album. We were really going for that "back shelf at Goodwill" vibe.

Alex Lupo (Vocals, Guitar) Riley Hanus (Vocals, Mandolin, Guitar) Matthias Agne (Bass, Baritone) Robert Free (Drums, Percussion) Blake Rason (Violin, Viola)
